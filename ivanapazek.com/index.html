Bio

After graduating from a challenging program at the University of Mathematics and beginning a career in software development in Croatia, Ivana found herself having what many wanted, but not feeling fulfilled.

Things started to change when she began to explore not only the aspects of her mind, but also the infinite aspects of her higher being. This led her on a journey of spiritual depths, inner discovery and principles about how to live the life of her dreams, as well as teaching this knowledge to others. She became a Law of Attraction life coach and with her profound yet simple teachings, has helped people from Japan to Nigeria, and Germany to the US, to make their dream lives a reality.

She currently lives with her husband in a peaceful part of Zagreb, Croatia, joyfully anticipating life's next adventure.

PROGRAMS

Ivana’s programs are dedicated to help people find their life purpose and live their ideal lives. These are people who have a whole list of accomplishments, but nothing that speaks to their soul, and says: You have done something amazing with your life! You are magnificent! They know that they want more, but feel trapped in their everyday realities and don’t know how to make their inner dreams a reality. What’s more, they are in the environment that doesn’t support a new vision for their life.

Find your Joy-factor is a step-by-step program to finding your life’s purpose, the very thing that brings the greatest joy in our lives. Finding our life purpose is the key component in living our ideal lives, and without it we will not feel fulfilled.

Manifest your Ideal Life is a program made to follow through with the book Lessons from Snow White. We use hidden spiritual messages from the tale Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs to learn how to manifest our ideal lives.

Once we realize, both the process of how to find our joy-factor and how to live our ideal lives, we can use it many times throughout our life. Our life purpose is not just one, and our vision of ideal life changes once we manifest what we want.
